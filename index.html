Our Solutions

We prioritize supporting organizations and governments grow through our digital transformation services, providing tailored and impactful solutions by thoroughly understanding their unique needs, challenges, and long-term objectives.

See all solutions

CollegePort™

Empowering higher institutions, our advanced school portal solution stands as a comprehensive, robust, and user-friendly platform. It offers unparalleled tools and functionalities, automating academic and administrative processes to enhance efficiency, productivity, and foster student success.

ProGrid ERP

Empower your organization with streamlined automation, eliminating data silos and boosting productivity. Enhance administrative processes, ensure secure tracking and compliance, make informed decisions with real-time data, and drive transparency and accountability for unparalleled efficiency.

SchoolPort™

A streamlined solution for nursery, primary, and secondary school administrators. Our integrated platform simplifies student-centric processes, ensuring high performance, data intelligence, and user-friendly operations. Students and parents gain controlled access for efficient management of academic information, online payments, and essential updates.

Electronic Pension Mgt. System

Streamlining pension management with secure digitization. From efficient data storage to biometric capture, it tackles key challenges—aggregating data, proof of life verification, and pension payments. Compliance-driven, it ensures secure cloud-based data management, simplifies verification, and offers rule-based payment workflows for seamless administration.

Learning Mgt. System

Our web-based e-learning solution revolutionizes education, ensuring comfort and enhanced communication. Instructors easily upload materials, form groups, and promote efficient collaboration. With a focus on enriching learning experiences, our LMS empowers both instructors and learners for success.

CLAG™ Biometrics

Combat identity fraud with our smart biometric solution. Securely enroll and aggregate biometric data, providing organizations with a unique, data-driven service for authentic identification and verification. Tailored for businesses of all sizes, CLAG™ is your key to a credible and fraud-resistant biometric database.
