We are BlueTag Team

Our Journey Since 2014

BlueTag Technologies has been at the forefront of innovative software solutions for over a decade, transforming how organizations operate through cutting-edge technology.

2014

Founded with a vision to revolutionize educational technology in Nigeria, we launched our first school management system.

2017

Expanded our product offerings to include enterprise solutions and government systems, reaching over 100 clients nationwide.

2021

Introduced cloud-based solutions and mobile applications, helping organizations adapt to remote work environments.

2025

Presently expanding our reach across Nigeria with innovative solutions, serving diverse sectors and establishing BlueTag as a leading technology provider nationwide.

Our Mission

To empower organizations through innovative technology solutions that enhance efficiency, productivity, and growth.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of transformative software solutions in Africa, recognized for excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Excellence, Innovation, Integrity, Customer-Focus, and Collaboration guide everything we do at BlueTag Technologies.
